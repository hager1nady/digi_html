My Favorite Hobby: Drawing

Materials Needed

  • Sketchbook
  • Pencils (HB, 2B, 4B)
  • Eraser
  • Sharpener
  • Colored pencils or markers
  • Ruler

Steps to get started

  1. Choose a simple object or reference image.
  2. Lightly sketch the basic shapes first.
  3. Add details gradually.
  4. Erase unnecessary lines carefully.
  5. Add shading to create depth & dimension.
  6. Outline and finalize your drawing.

Terminology

Sketch
A quick & light drawing used as a draft before the final version
Shading
The technique of adding light & dark areas to create a 3D effect.
Outline
The outer line that defines the shape of an object.
Perspective
The method used to show depth & distance in art.
Contrast
The difference between light & dark areas in a drawing.
