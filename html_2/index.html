Grape Leaves

Ingredients

  • Grape leaves (about 500 grams).
  • Rice (½ to 1 cup, depending on quantity).
  • Chopped onion (1–2 small onions).
  • Chopped tomatoes (1–2 pieces).
  • Chopped parsley (¼ cup).
  • Fresh mint, chopped (1 tablespoon).
  • Lemon juice (3–4 tablespoons).
  1. Rinse the grape leaves well and blanch them in boiling water for 2 minutes. Then drain and let them cool.
  2. Wash the rice thoroughly until the water runs clear.
  3. In a bowl, mix the rice, chopped onions, tomatoes, parsley, mint, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, pepper, and spices.
  4. Place one grape leaf flat, shiny side down, and put a small spoonful of the filling near the stem end.
  5. Roll the leaf tightly, folding in the sides, to form a neat little parcel.
  6. Arrange the stuffed leaves tightly in a large pot, seam side down.
  7. Pour water or broth over the leaves until they are just covered. Place a plate on top to keep them from unraveling.
  8. Cover the pot and simmer on low heat for about 45 minutes to 1 hour until the rice is cooked and tender.
  9. Remove from heat and let cool slightly before serving.
